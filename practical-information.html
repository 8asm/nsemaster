Frequently asked questions


What is the application process?

The required documents to apply online are:
- CV/Resume
- Motivation/Cover letter
- Copy of your Passport/ID Card
- ID picture
- Reference letter(s)
- Transcripts of records
- Copy of the last degree awarded
- English Language Certificate

It is compulsory for international students to follow the Campus France procedure in parallel.

How much are the tuition fees?

See in the following pages the present enrollment fees:
- At the ECL
- At INSA Lyon
- At the University Claude Bernard Lyon 1

Are there scholarships available?

The selection committee of the Master NSE will take into consideration all the applications, to allocate a few Université de Lyon excellence scholarships. There is no separate scholarship application.

The program can neither offer any Eiffel scholarship nor research/teaching assistantship for 2020-2021.

Can I get an accommodation on campus?

International Masters’students are given the opportunity to live on campus. M1 students have priority. If the selection committee select your application and you confirm your will to join us, you will be sent a form to book a room in one of the residences.
